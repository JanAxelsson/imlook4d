<h1>Show patient info</h1>
<HR></HR>
<!------------------->
	<P>
	When a check-mark is on the "Show patient name" preference, information related to the patient will be displayed on a copied image.
	</P>

	<P>  
	The information is the same as in the imlook4d window title   
	For DICOM images, this information is derived from the patient name.
	For ECAT and SHR images, the file name is displayed, which may or may not contain patient information.
	
	</P>

	<P> 
	This preference is not remembered when opening a new window using Open menu.
	</P>

	<P> 

	See Copy Image
	</P>
