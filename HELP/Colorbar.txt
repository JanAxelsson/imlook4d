<h1>Colorbar</h1>
<HR></HR>
<!------------------->
	<P>
	Displays the colorbar.  
	Colorscales can be changed by "Color" menu, or by right mouse-click on Colorbar.   
	</P>
	
	<P>
	Shifting the colormap is toggled on/off by right mouse-click on Colorbar, followed by selecting "Interactive colormap shift". 
	<B>It is important to turn off "Interactive colormap shift"</B> when done modifying the colorbar; otherwise, it interferes with the GUI.
	Selecting a new color scale from the Edit menu resets the previously peformed interactive colormap shifts.
	</P>
