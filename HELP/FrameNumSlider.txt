<h1>Frame slider</h1>
<HR></HR>
<!------------------->
	<P>
	Sets the frame number.  Pressing the CTRL-key or SHIFT-key while using the mouse scroll-wheel can also be used to move up and down the frames.
	</P>
	
	<P>
	When "PC Image" radio-button is selected, this slider is used to set the principal component number.  
	</P>
	
		
	<P>
	See also "PC Image" radio-button.
	See also "Frame" edit box.
	</P>
