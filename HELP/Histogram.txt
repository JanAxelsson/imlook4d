<h1>Histogram</h1>
<HR></HR>
<!------------------->
	<P>
	Displays a histogram of all pixels in currently displayed image.
	</P>

	<P>
	The histogram display the frequency of all pixels in image.  
	Pixels below display range (colorbar levels) are displayed in white histogram bars.
	Pixels within display range are histogrammed in red, and pixels above display range are in blue.
	Statistics is also printed in graph.
	</P>
	
	<img src="Images\histogram.png" alt="Histogram image"/> 
	
	<P>
	The ranges below, within and above displayed window levels are also indicated with horisontal lines.
	It may be useful to use the Matlab zoom (looking glas with + button) to better see the lower frequency values, 
	that typically occur at the high pixel values.
	</P>
