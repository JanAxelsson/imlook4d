<h1>Export filtered</h1>
<HR></HR>
<!------------------->

	<P>
	Exports the <B>manipulated</B> (filtered) data to MATLAB workspace.
		<UL>
			<LI> The image data is manipulated by filters or models.
			<LI> The image data is <B>not</B> manipulated by settings changing the color scale (scale min/max, remove negatives, invert).
		</UL>
	</P>




	<P>
	Exports all the internal variables of the current imlook4d instance to the MATLAB workspace.  
	Variables may be used for subsequent analysis, and can be imported using the "Workspace/Import" menu.
	</P>
	

	<P>
	<a href="export_details.html">Detailed information</a> 

	</P>

	
	<P>
	See the Import to workspace feature, to reload manipulated variables from workspace to imlook4d. 
	</P>
