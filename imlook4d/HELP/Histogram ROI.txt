<h1>Histogram ROI</h1>
<HR></HR>
<!------------------->
	<P>
	Displays a histogram of pixels values from the currently selected ROI.
	</P>

	<P>
	The histogram display the frequency of the ROI pixel values. If a 4D image, the pixel data is from the currently selected frame.  


	The x-axis range is defined by the color bar limits.
	</P>
