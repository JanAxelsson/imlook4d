<h1>Copy to linked ROI </h1>
<HR></HR>
<!------------------->
	<P>
	All imlook4d windows with a button [A], [B] or [C] pressed, are linked in a group.  
	</P>
	
	<P>
	This menu copies the ROI to all linked images.  
	
	</P>
	
	<P>
	ROI names are updated.  ROI-names will overwrite previous names, if different.
	Also Visibility and Locking of ROIs are copied.

	</P>
