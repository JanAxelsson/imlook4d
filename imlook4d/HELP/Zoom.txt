<h1>Zoom Image </h1>
<HR></HR>
<!------------------->
	<P>
	Zooms an image with a fixed factor.  The Pan tool ("hand" toolbar button) can be used to move the image sideways.
	</P>
	
	
	<P>
	Example zoom factors are:
	
	
		<UL>
			<LI> <B>Constant number </B> <BR> 
				  2
			<LI>  <B>Formula </B> <BR> 
				   5/3
		</UL>
	</P>
