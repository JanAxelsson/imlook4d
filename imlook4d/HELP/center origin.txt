<h1>Center origin</h1>
<HR></HR>
<!------------------->
	<P>
		Centers the origin in a nifti file, using SPM routines
	</P>

	<P> 
        Sometimes a registration between images fails because the coordinate system differs to much.  This routines sets the center of the image to origin.
	</P>
