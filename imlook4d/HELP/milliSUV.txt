
<h1>milliSUV calculation Script</h1>
<HR></HR>
<!------------------->
	<P>This script generates a new volume with milliSUV (1000 x Standardized Uptake Value) for PET, instead of Bq/ml uptake.  

    </P><P>The milliSUV value is calculated based on injected activity and body mass, taken from the DICOM file header.</P>
