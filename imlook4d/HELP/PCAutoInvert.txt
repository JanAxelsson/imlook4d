<h1>Auto checkbox</h1>
<HR></HR>
<!------------------->
	<P>
	Due to the nature of principal component analysis, principal component (PC) images may be randomly negative or positive.  
	When Auto checkbox is selected, an algorithm tries to determine the best way to visualize a PC image.
	The principal component PC Images will be automatically set to the most probable normal/inverted state.   
	</P>
