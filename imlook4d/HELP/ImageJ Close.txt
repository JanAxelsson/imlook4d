<h1>ImageJ Close</h1>
<HR></HR>

<!------------------->


<h2>Use</h2>

	<P>
		Closes ImageJ that was started from imlook4d.
	</P>
	<P>
		This may solve memory errors from previous ImageJ sessions.
	</P>
