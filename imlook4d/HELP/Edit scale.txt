<h1>Edit scale</h1>
<HR></HR>
<!------------------->
	<P>
	Interactively modifieds the low and high value of the image colorbar.  
	The slider range is based on the mininum and maximum values in currently displayed image.  
	</P>

	<P>
	If slider range is not sufficient, type a new value into the min- or max textbox.  
	</P>

	<P>
	The slider range can be made smaller, which may be useful if small changes are needed in an image with large span of pixel values.
    The range is zoomed in from the currently set min and max values, by pressing the button "Set min-max".    
	</P>

	<P>
	The default range can be reset by pressing the "Reset" button.  
	</P>


	<P>
	The "Auto colorscale" and "Remove negatives" radio-buttons in the imlook4d are cleared.   
	</P>
