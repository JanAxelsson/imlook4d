<h1>Measure Tool </h1>
<HR></HR>
<!------------------->
	<P>
	This tools measures lengths.  It is activated by pressing the measure button, and you click the start and end position.
The length is displayed in the image format's unit, which often is in millimeters.  The line representing the measurement is shown in the image.
	</P>

	<P>
The measured data is shown at the bottom of the imlook4d window, and also in the Matlab workspace.
	</P>
	
	<P>
The line may be removed by Shift-clicking, or Right-clicking on the line.
	</P>
