<h1>PC-image mode</h1>
<HR></HR>
<!------------------->
	<P>
	The Principal Component (PC) image.  The frame slider now works as a principal-component slider.  
	A PC-image consists of a weighted mean of the images in the current slice. 
	</P>
	
	<P> 
	The weight-factors are designed to give maximum variance in PC image 1.<BR>
	Omitting the first PC image, weight factors are designed to give maximum variance in PC image 2.<BR>
	Omitting the second PC image, .......
	</P>
	
	<P>
	See also "Image", and "Residual" radio-buttons.
	</P>
